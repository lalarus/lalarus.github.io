Designing to support user agency

In my Master Thesis, I explore and redesign design mechanisms on Instagram for a greater sense of user agency.

Published

August 1, 2023

TLDR
Problem

In today’s attention economy, digital interfaces undermine self-control. This results in detrimental effects on user’s well-being. I therefore explored how we can design interfaces that give back control (i.e. user agency) toward the people who interact with these interfaces.

Approach

Following a design thinking approach, I used desk research, probe studies and affinity mapping to explore how design mechanisms on Instagram support and undermine user control. Through experience prototyping, I then developed alternative design patterns that support sense of agency.

Solution

I identified six design patterns that describe how design mechanisms on Instagram support and undermine user control. I further developed 4 prototypes that redesign Instagram’s design mechanisms for greater user agency.
